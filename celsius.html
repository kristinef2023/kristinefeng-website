Lychee Glow

A delicately sweet and floral profile, bringing an exotic touch to your cocktail experience.

Lychee has been a favorite in cocktails for centuries, known for its refreshing sweetness that elevates any drink it pairs with. Whether you're sipping a gin and champagne mix or enjoying a chilled summer drink, Lychee is sure to add a touch of exotic flair.

  • 1 can Celsius Lychee
  • 1.5 oz gin
  • 0.5 oz elderflower liqueur
  • 0.5 oz fresh lime juice
  • 2-3 fresh lychees (or 0.5 oz lychee syrup)
  • Champagne (to top)
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add gin, elderflower liqueur, lime juice, and lychee syrup.
  2. Shake well and strain into a chilled coupe or martini glass.
  3. Top with Celsius Lychee for an energizing glow.
  4. Finish with a splash of champagne and garnish with a fresh lychee.
  5. Enjoy the perfect blend of refreshment and sophistication!

üç∏ Olive Twist Martini

A bold and refined take on the classic dirty martini, infused with Celsius Olive for a smooth yet invigorating twist.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Dry Vermouth
  • 2 oz Celsius Olive
  • 0.5 oz Olive Brine (adjust for taste)
  • Ice
  • Garnish: 3 Green Olives on a skewer

Instructions:

  1. Fill a mixing glass with ice and add vodka, dry vermouth, Celsius Olive, and olive brine.
  2. Stir (or shake for extra chill) until well combined.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  4. Garnish with three green olives on a skewer.
  5. Enjoy a perfectly balanced, modern martini with a refined twist.
