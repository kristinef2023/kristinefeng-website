Crust Away: Social Media Campaign

Category: Marketing Internship Assignment | Social Media Content

The Challenge

As part of the final stage for the Crust Away Marketing Internship, I was tasked with creating a social media post that embodies the brand’s voice and mission.

Brand Identity & Design Approach

Using the brand’s color palette—deep blue, vibrant orange, and crisp white—I designed a visually engaging post that highlights Crust Away’s unique selling points. The goal was to make the content both eye-catching and informative, ensuring it resonates with the target audience.

Reflections

This project challenged me to think critically about brand identity and audience engagement in a fast-paced digital landscape. It was a rewarding experience to translate Crust Away’s vision into an effective marketing asset.
