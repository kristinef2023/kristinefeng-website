January

Breakfast Month

Frankel's Delicatessen & Appetizing

Start your morning off right with a bagel from Frankel's! Better yet, top it off with some of their delicious cucumber salad!

Baby Blue's Luncheonette

This Greek style diner is serving up breakfast classics with a Mediterranean twist! Highly recommend their Zorba Plate!

Ceremonia Bakeshop

This breakfast burrito is stuffed with four scrambled eggs, potatos, chorizo, and melty cheddar cheese! Stop by and grab a mochi muffin as well!

Winson Bakery

Known for their famous BEC on a scallion pancake, and deservedly so! Make sure you also try their sticky red date cake or their red bean doughnut too!

La Cabra

This Cardamom Bun is surely worth the wait here! If they run out the Canele is also a great choice!

Radio Bakery

If you wake up early enough.. come down to Radio Bakery and make sure you are leaving with their Smoked Salmon Sandwich on everything spice foccacia!

February

Sweet Treats Month

Sant Ambroeus

Treat yourself like the princess you are with Sant Ambroeus' precious Princessa Cake. Beautiful and delicious!

Yuan Yang

Warm up or cool down with the Taro Mochi Pudding served your choice of hot or chilled!

Hani's Bakery

Your classic PB & J in a cake! Wild blueberries and delicious peanut butter frosting makes for a truly amazing bite!

Patisserie Vanessa

Dare I say the perfect French pastry? Find one of the best Canele's in the city in the Upper East Side at Vanessa's!

Luna Cafe & Bakery

This Pumpkin Bun is not only adorable and delicious, but also a steal! $2 for a bun means we will be back for more...

La Cantine

Cake in a jar! We got the hazulnut one, but check their Instagram as their desserts change daily!

March

Pi Month

Scarr's Pizza

If you find yourself in LES, find yourself a slice of this delicious Hotboi slice!

Steve's Authentic Key Lime Pie

From 3 inches to 10, Steve's got it all! Want it dipped in chocolate.. he's got that too!

Fini Pizza

Fini your night off with a delicious slice of this tomato pie! Topped with garlicky bread crumbs... you can't go wrong!

Pie Corps

Want smore? Pie Corps not only offers a scrumptions smore pie, there's cake as well!

Amore Pizza Caf√©

Can't pick a sauce? Good thing this pie has marinara, vodka, and pesto!

Petee's Pie Company

Go bananas for Petee's silky banana cream pie! No matter what option you choose, you'll be satisfied!

April

Vegetarian Month

Dialogue Coffee & Flowers

If you find yourself bored at home, bring yourself to this all vegan coffee and flower shop!

Dun-Well Doughnuts

Right off the Montrose L, the perfect vegan donuts are steps away! We love the classic Strawberry Sprinkle, but follow their Instagram to see their daily specials!

Superiority Burger

If you're lucky this Pearl Passionfruit Pie will be availible at Superiority Burger! Make sure you get their name sake burger and fries as well!

Planta Queen

Planta Queen is dishing up some of the best vegan sushi in the city! If you can, try to make it to one of their Maki Monday's for unlimited rolls for $29 after 5pm!

Bunna Cafe

This vegan Ethiopian restaurant is serving up some of the best Beyaynetu in the city! Our favorite item to get was the Misir Wot!

chloe.

This giant vegan cinnamon roll can only be acquired on a weekend, so make haste and grab one! Make sure you also get a side of their butternut mac n cheese!

May

Asian & Latin Month

Xi'an Famous Foods

These long and chewy hand-pulled noodles have multiple locations in NYC! Adjust your spice level to your liking, but our favorite is the Pork "Zha Jiang" at the spiciest level!

Son del North

This no rice burrito spot is one you should not miss. We love their torilla and their frijoles con queso with white beans!

Chef Katsu Brooklyn

If you find yourself in Brooklyn, it better be at Chef Katsu to get their Chicken Katsu Curry Bowl! Try out one of their delectable desserts as well!

Tikal Mayan Food

In the heart of Manhattan lies Tikal Mayan Food... get the Fried Red Snapper and get blown away!

Luna Cafe & Bakery

This may seem familiar if you remember February's Pumpkin Bun... the pastries are so cheap get yourself multiple! Including this Black Sesame filled Sesame Ball!

Taqueria Ramirez

The perect day in Greenpoint includes a trip to Taqueria Ramirez! We recommend the Al Pastor loaded with all the toppings!

June

Across Oceans Month

Roti Roll (Bombay Frankie)

You cannot come to Roti Roll obviously without getting their Frankies, but you absolutely must come with a friend to split their Samosas! Hydrate yourself too with a Mango Lassi!

Ten Ichi Mart

You'll be seeing a lot of Tenichi Mart here! We love their sushi.. especially their Salmon Nigiri!

OKONOMI Brooklyn

Breakfast and Lunch?? Okonomi offers a traditional Japanese breakfast in the AM, and turns into a Ramen restaurant at 5:30pm! Whichever option you choose will surely delight!

Larry's Ca Phe

This Vietnamese cafe is serving up some of the best drinks and pastries in Brooklyn! Our favorite is Larry's Green... coconut water topped with a fluffy matcha cream!

Ten Ichi Mart

We're back! But this time with Tuna Nigiri!

Nepali Momo Cafe

Find yourself the time to do a proper Momo crawl.. once you do you'll find that Nepali Momo Cafe is on the list! Make sure you try their Sel Roti that is crispy, savory, and served with a spicy dipping sauce!

July

Summer Month

The Commodore

On a hot summer month.. or any day of the year, get yourself a Frozen Mojito over at The Commodore! Goes great with their Nachos or Tuna Melt!

L&B Spumoni Gardens

Come here for their pizza, stay for the ice cream! This classic soft serve goes great with their famous Sicilian pie!

Morgenstern‚Äôs Finest Ice Cream

With 50 flavors to choose from, everyone has something to love at Morganstern's! If you're feeling boozy, get one of their specialty shakes...

Ray's Candy Store

Open 24 hours, and still run by Ray, this famous candy shop is home to some of the best Soft Serve in the city! Don't sleep on their Beignets or their Fried Oreos either!

Dog Day Afternoon

With two locations in the city, Dog Day Afternoon is serving up delicious dogs! Ranging from Polish Kielbasa to Chicago to even Vegan dogs, this place serves up something for everyone!

L&L Hawaiian Barbecue

L&L is dishing up some of the best Hawaiian BBQ in the city! We love the Lava Chicken Plate, but make sure you try their Spam Musubi as well!

August

Sandwich Month

Saiguette

If you find yourself on the Upper West Side, get yourself the best Bahn Mi in New York! The Grilled Lemongrass Pork Shoulder is our favorite! If you come here you also must order their BBQ Pork Bun and Laska!

Evil Katsu

In the heart of East Village, you will find Evil Katsu. Serving up one of the best Japanese Sandos in the city, you must acquire their Chicken Katsu on the softest milk bread you will ever bite into!

Emmy Squared Pizza

Don't be fooled by the name of Emmy Squared Pizza when I tell you that the best thing on the menu is the coveted Emmy Burger. Served on a pretzle bun with carmelized onions and Emmy sauce, you'll leave wanting more! Make sure to come early as they only make 25 a day!

BK Jani

This Pakistani burger spot is serving up some of our favorite classics with flair! Their namesake burger is topped with mint chutney, raita, and a grilled tomato! A must order is a side of their addictive Masala Fries!

S&P Lunch

This classic lunch counter is home to the best Tuna Melt in the city! Sit down and order, and pay when you're done! If it's a cozy day, get yourself a nice bowl of Matzah Ball Soup as well!

Salty Lunch Lady's Little Luncheonette

A hidden gem in Ridgewood lies one of the best veggie sandwiches. Salty Lunch Lady's Squash Me Sandwich holds roasted butternut squash, kale pesto, goat cheese ricotta, and a deliciously dressed radicchio! The menu changes daily, and they constantly rotate their desserts! Highly recommend a trip down!

September

Diner Month

Three Decker Diner

Right off Nassau Ave you will find the coziest diner in NYC. Three Decker Diner has a menu large enough to satsify your entire party! We love everything on their menu, but you truly can never beat a classic milkshake to satisfy that sweet tooth!

Agi's Counter

This Hungarian counter is home to one of the best slices of cheesecake in the city. Paired with olive oil and lemon, this rich and creamy dessert is not one to miss. For a lil extra, you can get it topped with their delicious blueberry compote!

Thai Diner

Thai Diner is serving up one of the best takes on a breakfast sandwich. Their egg and cheese is accompanied with Sai Oua Herbal Sausage & Thai Basil all wrapped in their delicious Roti! Don't you dare forget to have a Thai Iced Tea to pair on the side!

B&H Dairy

One of the last kosher dairy counters in the city is home to the best cheap, late night eats! Of course you have to get their Tuna Melt on Challah, but do not skip out on their Latkes!

Brooklyn Farmacy & Soda Fountain

This classic ice cream joint has one of our favorite Banana Splits in the city! They are known for their classic sodas and egg creams, but we could eat their Sundaes all day!

Golden Diner

Famous for thei giant fluffy pancakes, Golden Diner is a secret contender for one of our favorite Tuna Melts. Topped with a whole bag of Salt and Vinegar chips, this crunchy melt is not one to sleep on!

October

Hidden Deals Month

Ten Ichi Mart

We are back at Ten Ichi! Not many people know that they also sell hot buns! Our favorite is undoubtly their Chicken Curry Bun! Order at the front and pick up in the back!

Edy's Grocer

Located in the heart of Greenpoint, Edy's Grocer is home to some of the best Lebanese staples for a great deal! We love grabbing their mezze, specifically their Chili Crisp Labneh or their Muhummara!

Hindu Temple Canteen

If you find yourself on a food crawl in Flushing... make the extra walk over to Hindu Temple Canteen! In the basement you will find some of the best South Indian snacks and quick bites! We recommend their weekend special of the Hyderabadi Masala Dosa and their Poori Masala! Don't leave without getting one of their Mango Lassi's, our favorite in the city!

Ten Ichi Mart

Back again! Ten Ichi is also home to our favorite Onigiri in the city! After 8:00pm is their deal of all their fresh food 50% off! Come 15 minutes early as everything goes by then!

Mama Yoshi Mini Mart

Located in Ridgewood, Mama Yoshi is known for their delicious Spicy Katsu Sando! The sleeper here is their Mac Salad! Get both and you will not be disappointed!

Deluxe Food Market

On Elizabeth Street, you will find one of the best deals in the city! Deluxe Food Market houses some of the best cheap eats, we recommend you get a bag of their Scallion Twist Buns and their Beef Chow Fun!

November

Family Style Month

Birds of a Feather

This Williamsburg restaurant is one of our favorite Family Style places! This classic Chinese restaurant is serving up large plates great for groups! We love their Pan Fried Pork Buns, but truly nothing on this menu is a miss!

Bernie's

On the corner of Driggs and Lorimer is Bernie's! Right next to McCarren, this could not be anymore of a perfect spot for their delicious Brownie Sundae! Known for their large serving sizes and strong drinks, this is a crowd-pleasing spot!

Ayat

This Palestinian spot is serving up the best authentic Middle Eastern favorites in NYC! We love everything from their Hummus to their Mansaf! Come with a large group and order one of everything!

Chama Mama

This Georgian staple has one of the best Khachapuri's in the city! The cheesy goodness is not only delicious, but a delightful experience! We love their Khinkali as well!

212 East

This late night spot has some of the best cheap drinks in the city, as well as a food menu that ranges from Kimchi Fried Rice to Fish and Chips!

Sappe

From the sister restaurant of Soothr, Sappe is serving some of the best Thai in NYC! Besides the gorgeous interior, we love their Khao Soi and Mama E-La!

December

Comfort Food Month

Pierozek

This quaint Polish restaurant is home to one of our favorite Pierogi's! From 9 flavors to choose from, you can have Pierogi's for lunch, dinner, and dessert! We love their Kielbasa dish that comes with traditional Polish Kielbasa, Three Pierogis of choice, served with red onion and pickles!

Menkoi Sato

This West Village favorite has a deliciously simple menu with a chicken broth base to all their ramens! We love their Spicy Shoyu, but here there are no wrong choices!

Frank

This cash-only Italian joint is home to one of our favorite dishes in the city! We love everything here, but their Spaghetti Limone is one to look out for!

Shuka

Right off MacDougal, you will find some of the best Middle Eastern bites in NYC! We recommend their Beet Hummus, but we recommend coming with a large group and ordering everything!

Yonah Schimmel's Knish Bakery

This tiny Jewish bakery is whipping out the best Knishes, ranging from sweet to savory, Yonah Schimmel has over 15 flavors to choose from! We love their classic potato, but a delightful suprise was their Red Cabbage Knish!

M Shanghai

This lowkey Williamsburg gem is dishing up one of our favorite soup dumplings in Brooklyn. We love everything from their Spicy Wontons to their Shanghai Style Lomein!
