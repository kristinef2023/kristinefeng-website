Split

This project was inspired by my love of diners, especially one my friend and I recently stumbled upon that brought back memories of growing up in the Midwest. Large menus always induce anxiety, as I need time to make a choice. I wanted to capture how even simple choices can feel overwhelming, yet fundamental rights, like those on a ballot, shouldn’t be. Growing up in Missouri, I felt compelled to advocate for women’s body autonomy and the right to choose.
