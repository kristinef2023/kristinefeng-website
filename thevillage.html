Overview

The Village produces dynamic, live events where emerging artists across all disciplines develop and share “artworks-in-progress” with engaged audiences. By uniting social connection with the creative process, The Village operates as an innovative home for artists, an enlivening community for audiences, and an accessible space for all. I joined the team to support the marketing and social media strategy, starting with Salons XII and XIII, with a focus on building long-term brand awareness, increasing engagement, and growing the community. My role involves content creation, event promotion, and strategic audience engagement, which continues to evolve.

Challenges

  • Building momentum for Salons XII and XIII while establishing a consistent long-term marketing strategy.
  • Increasing engagement and brand visibility across social platforms.
  • Developing a sustainable content strategy that maintains audience interest beyond events.

Strategy & Execution

  • Event Promotion & Real-Time Engagement – Creating pre-event hype, live updates, and post-event content to maximize reach.
  • Branding & Content Strategy – Establishing a consistent aesthetic and messaging approach.
  • Community Growth & Interaction – Engaging with followers through interactive posts, Q&As, and collaborations.
  • Analytics & Iteration – Tracking performance to continuously refine content strategy.

Results & Ongoing Impact

  • 4.64% percentage increase in followers since starting.
  • Growing community interaction, with an increase in comments, shares, and story engagement.
  • Successful turnout for Salons XII and XIII, with insights guiding future marketing efforts.
  • Continuing to develop and optimize marketing strategies for future events and overall brand presence.
